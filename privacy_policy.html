Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Family Safety ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Family Safety mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account.

1.2 Location Data

With your explicit consent, we collect precise location data (latitude and longitude) from devices running our app in "Child" mode. Location data is collected:

  • Every 20-30 seconds when the app is active
  • When the device moves more than 20 meters
  • Through a foreground service with a visible notification

1.3 Device Information

We collect battery level and online/offline status to help parents monitor device health.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Real-time location sharing: Allowing parents to see their children's location for safety purposes
  • Geofence alerts: Notifying parents when a child enters or leaves a designated safe zone
  • SOS alerts: Enabling children to send emergency alerts with their location to parents
  • Location history: Showing movement history within the family group

3. Information Sharing

Location and device information is shared only with members of the same family group. We do not:

  • Sell personal data to third parties
  • Share location data with advertisers
  • Use location data for any purpose other than family safety
  • Share data with any entity outside your family group

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely in Google Firebase Cloud Firestore with the following protections:

  • Encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access controlled by Firestore security rules
  • Only family members can access family data
  • Authentication required for all data access

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for family use and includes features for children. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • Children must explicitly consent before joining a family group
  • A visible notification is always shown when location sharing is active
  • Children can see that their location is being shared
  • Location sharing can be paused by the child at any time
  • We comply with applicable children's privacy laws

6. Background Location Access

Our app uses background location access to maintain real-time location sharing. This is necessary for the core safety functionality of the app. Important details:

  • Background location is used only for sharing location with family members
  • A persistent notification is always displayed when background location is active
  • Users are clearly informed before granting background location permission
  • Background location can be disabled at any time
  • We optimize battery usage by limiting update frequency

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Stop location sharing at any time
  • Leave a family group
  • Request data export

8. Data Retention

Location history is retained for up to 30 days. When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app.
